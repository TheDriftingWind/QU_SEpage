Overview

Our programs feature active, collaborative classrooms that support multiple teaching and learning styles, and feature the same powerful software and tools used by professionals. In the simply named Laptop Room, CS and software engineering students do more than stare at computer monitors, including bringing robots to life through the use of a neural network.
