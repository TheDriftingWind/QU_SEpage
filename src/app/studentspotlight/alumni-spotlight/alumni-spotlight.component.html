Alumni

Our Alumni have taken what they have learned through the Software Engineering program into their career paths and have shared their many accomplishments and experience after graduation.

Sarah Johnson

Working at Google

Hometown: Bridgeport, Connecticut
Graduation Date: May 2016
Company: Google
Department: Engineering Department
Current Position: Software Engineer

During her freshmen year at Quinnipiac, she decited to take an Intro to CS class, just to see what it was like, taking Quinnipiac's CSC110 on campus. In her words, "Almost immediately, I fell in love—it became my favorite class I’d ever taken, and I started spending a very large percentage of my time programming, watching lectures, etc." Fast-forward to her senior year, Sarah managed to land a position at Google.

She enjoys her position at Google because they’re making good use of her skillset she states. She feels as though she is actually contributing a lot to making decisions about things that do matter for our users in the end; as well as making steady forward progress on the smaller things. Sarah states "I enjoy at Google because it’s fun. I’m working on hard, interesting problems, and I’m working with really great people - people who can get the job done, who act rationally, who don’t make life difficult when it doesn’t have to be. I enjoy going to work."

Tom Hanks

Working at Cigna

Hometown: Long Island, New York
Graduation Date: May 2015
Company: Cigna
Department: Software Engineering and Innovation
Current Position: Software Engineer

At Cigna, the customers are at the center of everything that they do. "Every day, we strive to make our customers’ experiences easy, reliable, and personal in ways that are proactive and empathetic," Tom expresses.

Tom states that he has been provided with excellent opportunities to progress throughout the company and develop my skills since joining Cigna. He states, "There has always been a clear career progression path in front of me within each department, but at the same time there have always been opportunities to move within departments and utilise skills in a new environment if desired. My current role offers the chance to liaise with colleagues (both internal and external) throughout the company, which is interesting and allows me to experience new cultures and viewpoints."
