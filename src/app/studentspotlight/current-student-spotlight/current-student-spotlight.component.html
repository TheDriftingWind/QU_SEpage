Sam Smith

Aspirations in Computing Award


Hometown: Boston, Massachusetts
Minor: Psychology
Expected Graduation Date: May 2019
Extracurricular Activities: Softball, Computing Club, Big Event

Through persistence and hard work, Sam has made a great achievement of winning the Aspirations in Computing award. The national contest is sponsored by tech companies like Microsoft, Apple and Bank of America. Sam hopes the contest award will help her start the next phase of her tech career at Ohio State. After graduation, she said she plans to stay in Ohio and pursue a software developer position at a start-up company.

Sam's project, Euphoria, is a mental health study focusing on the potential effects of using virtual reality technology for treatment and relaxation. The project seeks to determine how virtual reality may be utilized for mental health treatments by inducing euphoric feelings through various interactions such as touch-induced wings, flight, and exploration of a virtual world.

Max Thompson

Activision Internship


Hometown: Hamden, Connecticut
Minor: Mathematics
Expected Graduation Date: May 2020
Extracurricular Activities: QUESO, Gaming Club, SPB

Max, an avid gamer, landed his dream internship at his favorite video game publisher company, Activision. The internship was a 12-week program where Max was placed on a team and worked on a project for an upcoming game. The program provided him housing and compensation for traveling expenses since he relocated to California for the summer.

"As an intern, you get to attent different networking events such as baseball games, studio visits, community service, hiking, movie nights, dinners, and an E3 mixer," says Max. Max also recieved many perks such as branded company giveaways and discounted copies of their games. Max hopes to go back to Activision to work as a full-time employee.
